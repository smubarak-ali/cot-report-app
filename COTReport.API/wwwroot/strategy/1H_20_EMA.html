• Strategy Setup:
    • 20 EMA (close, shift 0) on 1H timeframe
    • 12 and 26 EMA's (close, shift 0) on 5M timeframe
    • Only Pre-London, London and New York sessions
    • Optional - MACD with only histogram and default settings on 5M only
  • Long Trade Setup:
    • Plot 20EMA on 1H chart and wait for the price to go either above or below the EMA. Also, look for the slope in EMA, that way we will get the help from the trend
    • Once a slope is spotted, drop down to 5M timeframe and plot 12 and 26 EMA's and MACD
    • Wait for the 12 EMA to go above 26 EMA and also they are fanned out i.e. they have distance in between
    • If MACD is applied then check if the red histogram is smaller and soon the green bar will appear
    • Once the criteria matches, enter the trade with 1:2 RR
  • Short Trade Setup:
    • Inverse / opposite of the long setup
  • Recommendation:
    • Do not risk more than 0.5% per trade
    • Set a number of max trades criteria. For example, if first trade wins then stop trading for the rest of the day. Stop trading when two SL hits in a day, etc.
    • Avoid trading news and exit all the positions before
